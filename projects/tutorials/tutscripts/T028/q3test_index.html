The level was converted with the luxf3d tool and is loaded as l3dlevelmodel, which generates an octree from it and applies the lightmaps.

Authors of the level (textures were removed):
Alcatraz (http://www.planetquake.com/bighouse),
Nunuk ( http://www.planetquake.com/nunuk),
Sock (http://www.planetquake.com/simland)


Use W+A+S+D+SPACE+C and your mouse to fly around, SHIFT accelerates.

You can hide this by pressing ENTER
